StackSight

Your browser, finally organised.

Group the sites you actually use into stacks. See how often you visit each one. Spot what you're paying for and not opening.

THREE STEPS
  1. 1
    Visit a site you use regularly.
    A small prompt appears in the bottom-right corner.
  2. 2
    Pick a stack — or create one.
    Stacks are your own categories. "Work", "Banking", "Reading", whatever fits.
  3. 3
    Open the popup any time with Ctrl + Shift + S
    Search, hit Enter, and you're there. Muscle memory in two days.
WHAT YOU GET
Quick launcher
Type a few letters, press Enter. Faster than tab-hunting.
Cost-per-visit
"$15/mo, 1 visit" becomes "$15/visit." The picture is unmissable.
Cancellation candidates
A "Stale" tab appears when you have paid items you haven't used.
Renewal alerts
A small banner if a paid service renews in the next 7 days.
Your data stays with you.

Nothing leaves your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics, no telemetry. Removing the extension wipes everything permanently.
